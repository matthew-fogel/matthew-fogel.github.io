Links to websites and articles I find interesting:

  • Google Ngram Viewer

    This tool allows users to track the frequency that terms appear in the English language from 1500 to present. A guide to using the tool can be found here.

  • Best of the Web

    An old-fashioned internet directory that is curated by real humans.

  • ISS Spotter

    See when the International Space Station is over top of your city.

  • The Culturomics Paper

    A scientific research paper by Michel et al. in the January 2011 issue of Science. The authors use the data set from the Google Ngram corpus of books to quantitatively analyze changes in human culture over several centuries.
